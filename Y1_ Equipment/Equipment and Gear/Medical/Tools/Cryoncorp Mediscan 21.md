Model: Cryoncorp Mediscan 21
Type: Medscanner

**Game Notes:** 
> The difficulty level for using this scanner is the same as that for using a medpac – **Easy** for Wounded, **Moderate** for Incapacitated and **Difficult** for Mortally Wounded characters. Successful use of a medscanner **adds +1D to first aid rolls**.
