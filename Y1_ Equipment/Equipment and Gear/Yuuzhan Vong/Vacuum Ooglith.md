Model: Yuuzhan Vong Vacuum Ooglith
Type: Organic vacuum suit
Cost: Not available for sale
Availability: 4, X

> **Game Notes:** 
> The vaccuum ooglith is a variant of the ooglith cloaker that works in the vacuum of space. The vacuum ooglith only protects the wearer against the rigors of space, and in no way does it provide the wearer with any oxygen supply. Typically used in conjunction with a lungworm.

*Source: The New Jedi Order Sourcebook (pages 21-22)*
